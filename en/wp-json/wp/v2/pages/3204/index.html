The fourth Gravelion was held near the village of Hoverla (Zakarpattia Oblast).
The event impressed with its scale - there were more than 200 participants, and it seems that there were as many spectators!<\/p>

We even had to find additional camping spot to fit everyone!
Upper camp - for the party, lower camp - for the chill.<\/p>

Route itself was kind of a surprise for everyone - it followed mostly the freshly built gravel roads\u00a0(during 2015-2018)<\/span>.
