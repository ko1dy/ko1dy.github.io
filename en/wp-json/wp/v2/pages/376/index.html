HOW IT STARTED<\/h2>\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

It was 2018. We were returning from\u00a0a Poland-Slovakia touring ride<\/a>\u00a0with a final destination at the Shypit waterfall. The last part of the route ran along unmarked road from Uzhok to Volovets villages.<\/p>

To our surprise, instead of boring tarmac we got dozens of kilometers of gravel roads adventure with amazing views. Gravel roads are pretty rare in Ukraine(or that's what we thought...). For most of us, it was the first introduction to what we later called\u00a0#purecarpathiangravel<\/span><\/p>

We felt the irresistible urge to share the discoveries with our friends and let them feel what we felt. That's how the Gravelion was started - the very first gravel event in Ukraine.<\/span><\/p>\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/section>\n\t\t\t\t

GRAVELION<\/h2>\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

Every August starting from 2018, we hold GRAVELION.\u00a0<\/p>

Main goal is to gather friends from different parts of the country in a camp far away from civilized world. Camp afterparty is as important as a race itself (or even more?). Every time it is full of its own stories and great memories.<\/span><\/p>

Second goal is to explore the Carpathian mountains. We find new routes and locations each year, so it's a surprise for participants every time.<\/span><\/p>

And of course finally - it's rad and fun racing!<\/span><\/p>\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/section>\n\t\t\t\t
